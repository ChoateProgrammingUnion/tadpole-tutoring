Our Mission

Tadpole Tutoring offers affordable, high quality tutoring to students all over the world. We have a profound commitment to charity, donating 100% of our profits to reputable, charitable organizations in need.

We seek to be the friends, role models, and educators that help students find meaning in what they do and challenge themselves. All our tutors are thoughtful, highly talented individuals. They are the soul of who we are.
