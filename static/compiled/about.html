Our Mission

Empathy is the keystone of discovery. The Coronavirus has had an unprecedented effect upon the socioeconomic condition of nations across the globe. As the world economy falters and individuals struggle to stay healthy, the practice of social distancing has become a normality. This has resulted in a myriad of issues, notably a decrease in the quality of studentsâ€™ education. Young people are not only missing out on key face to face time with their teachers, but, perhaps more importantly, they are missing out on interactions with other individuals their age-- experiences that most anyone will tell you are fundamental to stimulating the learning process and igniting an enthusiasm for their work. It is through working with other students that kids find meaning in what they do, consider new perspectives, and challenge themselves. As such, in a time where the educational pillar that is the student community is crumbling, we seek to be the friends, role models, and educators that not only further the knowledge of students, but provide valuable interaction in a way that only youth can.
